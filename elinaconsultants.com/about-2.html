Vision

Evolve as a prestigious professional setup in a wide ranging service spectrum as premier solution providers; keeping up with global standards of solution delivery.
ELINA CONSULTANTS is a cluster of diverse professional services that provides Educational solutions, Strategic marketing consulting, Accounts & book keeping, corporate content writing and technological management services in IT sector to help individuals, companies & institutions measurably improve their performances.

Bringing together people, processes & technology under a single banner ELINA, we work with private businesses, service sector companies, corporate education brands, career seeking students, teaching academies, colleges and universities to help them improve results by achieving their target outputs.

Thousands of students, scores of private businesses and corporate education setups have been benefitted as a result of their association with our multi-purpose capacity resource-pool, constituent services and better educational experiences.

Launched in 2010, ELINA services meet the stringent requirements of the competitive times and rapid technological demands of 21st century, while providing a multi-sector SOLUTION platform by shaping a unique convergence of creative human resources, trans segment market collaborations and imaginative use of Information – communication technologies .

Our top focus remains to deliver a highly satisfying client experience to foster long term engagements with our continually expanding client base and partnering organizations.
